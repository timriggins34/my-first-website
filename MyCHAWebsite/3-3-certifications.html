DGFT & Regulatory Consultancy

Navigating the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) regulations requires specialized knowledge. We help businesses procure necessary licenses, ensuring you receive the maximum benefits under the current Foreign Trade Policy.

AEO Certification →

Authorized Economic Operator (AEO) status grants your business faster customs clearances, deferred payment of duties, and direct port delivery. We guide you through the complex application and audit process to secure your T1, T2, or T3 status.

SVB Registration →

If you are importing goods from related parties or foreign parent companies, Special Valuation Branch (SVB) procedures are mandatory. We handle SVB questionnaires, representations, and final order issuance to prevent unnecessary duty loading.

EPCG & Advance Licences →

We assist in procuring and redeeming Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) licenses and Advance Authorizations, allowing you to import capital goods and raw materials at zero customs duty based on export obligations.

MOOWR Scheme →

Manufacture and Other Operations in Warehouse (MOOWR) enables duty deferment on imported capital goods and inputs used for domestic manufacturing. We handle the application, bonding, and compliance required to set up under this scheme.
